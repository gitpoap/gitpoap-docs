# How to mint your GitPOAPs

<ImageContainer>
  <img
    src={require('@site/static/img/gitpoap_visuals/how-to-mint.gif').default}
    style={{
      maxWidth: '100%',
      textAlign: 'right',
    }}
  />
</ImageContainer>

- Navigate to [gitpoap.io](https://www.gitpoap.io/)
- Connect the wallet that you want to mint your GitPOAPs to.
- Depending on how you're eligible (Github activity, Ethereum address, email address), you'll connect either your Github or your email by clicking the blue `CONNECT ACCOUNTS` button on the top right.<br/>
  _If you don't know which you're eligible through, you can check at [gitpoap.io/eligibility](https://www.gitpoap.io/eligibility)_
- If you're eligible to collect a GitPOAP, the `CONNECT ACCOUNTS` button will turn into a `VIEW & MINT` button.
- Click that and hit "Mint". It'll mint to your connected Ethereum wallet!
- You can check to make sure it minted at `gitpoap.io/p/[your Ethereum address]`
