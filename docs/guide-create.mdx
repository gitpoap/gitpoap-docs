# Creating a GitPOAP

:::tip
For help on whether to choose **Custom GitPOAPs** or **Github-based GitPOAPs**, you can read about the [Types of GitPOAPs](./types-gp)
:::

import Tabs from '@theme/Tabs';
import TabItem from '@theme/TabItem';

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="custom" label="Custom GitPOAPs" default>
    <ImageContainer>
      <img
        src={require('@site/static/img/gitpoap_visuals/guide-create-custom.gif').default}
        style={{
          maxWidth: '100%',
          textAlign: 'right',
        }}
      />
    </ImageContainer><br />
    <h2> To create a Custom GitPOAP: </h2>
    <ul>
      <li> Navigate to gitpoap.io</li>
      <li> Connect your Ethereum wallet</li>
      <li> Click on "ISSUE GITPOAPS"</li>
      <li> Choose "CUSTOM"</li>
      <li> Upload your artwork, making sure that it fills the entire hexagon (please refer to our <a href="#">Design Guide</a>)</li>
      <li> Give your GitPOAP a descriptive name</li>
      <li> Write an informative description of the accomplishment that your GitPOAP is recognizing</li>
      <li> Enter the accomplishment period</li>
      <li> Fill in the email where you'll receive information about this GitPOAP</li>
      <li> Add in any contributors (you can always add more later!)</li>
      <li> Wait for your GitPOAP request to be approved!</li>
    </ul>

  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="github" label="Github-based GitPOAPs">
    <ImageContainer>
      <img
        src={require('@site/static/img/gitpoap_visuals/guide-create-github.gif').default}

    <h2> To create a Github-based GitPOAP: </h2>
    <ul>
      <li> Navigate to gitpoap.io</li>
      <li> Connect your Ethereum wallet</li>
      <li> Click on "ISSUE GITPOAPS"</li>
      <li> Choose "GITHUB-BASED"</li>
      <li> Connect your Github account</li>
      <li> Choose the repos that you want to recognize<br/>
        <i><b>Note:</b> If you don't see the repos you're looking for here, feel free to reach out to us at team@gitpoap.io</i></li>
      <li> Press "NEXT" and let us know if you'd like us to create the artwork if your team would like to submit artwork.<br/>
        <i><b>Note:</b> If you'd like us to design, please include any branding assets that our design team can use to create the artwork</i><br/>
        <i><b>Note:</b> If your team is creating the artwork, please check out our <a href="#">Design Guide</a>. You'll send us the design to team@gitpoap.io</i>
      </li>
      <li> Upload your artwork, making sure that it fills the entire hexagon (please refer to our <a href="#">Design Guide</a>)</li>
      <li> Give your GitPOAP a descriptive name</li>
      <li> Write an informative description of the accomplishment that your GitPOAP is recognizing</li>
      <li> Enter the accomplishment period</li>
      <li> Fill in the email where you'll receive information about this GitPOAP</li>
      <li> Add in any contributors (you can always add more later!)</li>
      <li> Wait for your GitPOAP request to be approved!</li>
    </ul>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
